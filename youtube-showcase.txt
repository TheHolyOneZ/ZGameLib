TITLE:
ZGameLib — Free Personal Game Library Manager | Full Feature Walkthrough


DESCRIPTION:
A complete walkthrough of ZGameLib, a free and open source personal game library manager
built with Tauri, Rust, and React. Manage all your games in one place — no cloud required,
everything runs locally on your machine.

In this video I walk through every tab and show what the app can do in under 2 minutes.

──────────────────────────────────────────
 FEATURES
──────────────────────────────────────────
🎮  Auto-scan for Steam, Epic Games, GOG & custom/emulator games
📊  Playtime tracking with a live weekly chart (last 12 weeks)
🖼  Cover art management — set custom covers or pull from scanner
⭐  Ratings, tags, notes, and favorite marking per game
📈  Stats page — total playtime, platform breakdown, lowest rated,
    most neglected, and more
🎰  Spin wheel — let the app pick what you play next
🔍  Command palette (Ctrl+K) for quick actions across the library
⌨  Full keyboard shortcut support
🎨  Multiple themes, accent colors, and cover display styles
🗂  Filter and sort by platform, tag, rating, playtime, and more
💾  100% local — no account, no telemetry, no internet required

──────────────────────────────────────────
 LINKS
──────────────────────────────────────────
🌐 Website & Download:  https://zsync.eu/zgamelib/
💻 GitHub:              https://github.com/TheHolyOneZ/ZGameLib

Free, open source, and Windows native. Built with Tauri + Rust on the backend
and React + TypeScript on the frontend for a fast, lightweight experience.
